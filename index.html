CRW11 – Central Rebuild Warehouse

Hub for bulk purchasing, storage, and kitting. Kits hold 7,000+ components and are QA-checked before shipment. Image spaces are ready for floor, kitting, and refurbishment photography.

  • Refurbishes Dematic SL2 and Intellisort slats in-house; industrial washer planned for 2026 to raise weekly capacity from ~384 to 1,600+ slats.
  • Bulk kitting, labeling, and staging accelerate on-site execution.
  • AAA/AAB quality tiers cataloged for reservation against uptime windows.
