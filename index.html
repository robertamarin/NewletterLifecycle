Program overview

Lifecycle leadership for our most critical sorters

The Asset Lifecycle program is run by the MHE RME Central Team – a group of rebuild and reliability experts who manage high-impact sorters so they run safely, predictably, and longer than OEM expectations.

What we do

Build and execute playbooks for complex sorter rebuilds, coordinate SMEs, kit every part, and commission equipment so networks avoid unplanned failures.

Full rebuild vs. major repair

Full rebuilds are complete tear-downs with recommissioning and QA; major repairs surgically stabilize critical subsystems to prevent downtime until the next rebuild window.

Reliability promise

Protect throughput, extend asset life, and reduce OEM dependency with standardized methods, vetted parts, and expert oversight.

CRW11 – Central Rebuild Warehouse

CRW11 is the hub for bulk purchasing, storage, refurbishment, and kitting. We prep thousands of components, QA every kit, and stage logistics so sites can execute rebuilds quickly.

  • Refurbishes Dematic and Intelligrated slats; Ambaflex and Intralox components are staged for high-velocity shipping.
  • Industrial washer investment planned to increase weekly capacity from ~384 to 1,600+ slats.
  • AAA/AAB quality tiers cataloged so sites can reserve kits ahead of critical uptime windows.
