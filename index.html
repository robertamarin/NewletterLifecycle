Program Milestones and Reliability

Intelligrated momentum

Thirteen full Intellisort rebuilds executed without OEM reliance (up from two in 2024), reducing vendor dependency and protecting sortation uptime.

Intralox rebuild program

Dedicated lifecycle PM and bulk parts discount enabled 88 Intralox rebuilds and more than $19M in savings.

European expansion

EU lifecycle PM hired; 14 European rebuilds delivered with $1.5M in savings using the North America playbook.

Dematic SC3 firsts

Standardized kits and CRW11 support produced the first SC3 rebuilds, boosting reliability and cost avoidance.

Reliability impact

Critical 1 uptime focus with standardized playbooks reduced unplanned downtime and protected throughput across every portfolio.

CRW11 – Central Rebuild Warehouse

CRW11 is the hub for bulk purchasing, storage, and kitting of sorter parts. Each kit contains more than 7,000 components and is verified before shipment to ensure rebuild success. In-house refurbishment of Dematic SL2 and Intellisort slats lowers costs and supports sustainability.

  • Bulk kitting, labeling, and staging accelerate on-site execution.
  • Refurbished slats flow from CRW11 to protect uptime while avoiding OEM lead times.
  • Industrial parts washer planned for 2026 will raise weekly slat refurbishment capacity from ~384 to 1,600+ and reduce ergonomic risk.
