CRW11 – Central Rebuild Warehouse

CRW11 is the hub for bulk purchasing, storage, and kitting of sorter parts, managing over seven thousand components per rebuild. In-house refurbishment of Dematic SL2 and Intellisort slats drives cost avoidance and sustainability while keeping Critical 1 uptime protected.

  • Average kit includes more than 7,000 components staged and verified.
  • Slat refurbishment throughput keeps rebuilt slats flowing without OEM lead-time risk.
  • Industrial parts washer arriving in 2026 lifts weekly slat capacity four-fold.
  • Improved ergonomics by shifting heavy cleaning tasks into automated cycles.
